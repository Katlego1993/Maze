THE MAZE PROJECT

To Create a Game in 3D using raycasting. Designed for kids so they can improve their spatial recognition and analytical skills.

Download and try the game on Scratch

ALL ABOUT THE MAZE PROJECT

For the end of my ALX/Holberton School foundational trimesters, I had to make my own portfolio project. What inspired me to work on Maze-Game was learning about game development. We are living in the most gaming-conscious era in human history, with the gaming industry raking in billions of dollars annually from sales and other gaming-related ventures... Realistic games are currently being played daily by a large number of individuals worldwide on their computers and phones. Personally, I've been into gaming since I was in my early teens, both on computers and on mobile devices. Because of my expertise with games, I find myself fascinated by the backstories of some of my favourite titles. I jumped at the chance to learn how to make a game
